High Cost of Abortion

Human Rights, Health

Legislative changes have furthered financial barriers individuals encounter when seeking abortion care, including procedure fees, travel expenses, and lost wages due to time off work. Limited insurance coverage and restrictive laws further exacerbate the burden, disproportionately affecting marginalized communities. These costs underscore the urgent need for accessible and affordable reproductive healthcare options for all individuals.
