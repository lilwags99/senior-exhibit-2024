Child & Youth Safety Online

Social, Mental Health

Online kids are exposed to harmful content and opinions. The majority of adolescent girls have appearance based insecurities. This is exacerbated by social media filters and comparisons. 90% of girls use filters or edits to alter their photos, impacting their mental health and body perception. We need to be aware of how we, ourselves, post and comment. We are the role models for the younger generation and we need to start acting like one.
