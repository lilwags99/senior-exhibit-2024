Tree Canopies and Urban Heat Island

Environmental

Each year global temperatures are increasing, and the asphalt, concrete and metals that make up our streets and buildings only magnify these temperatures in cities and towns. Trees offer us natural protection from the heat and studies show that they can reduce temperatures when we have enough canopy cover. Although many cities and towns have pledged to increase their canopy coverage, many states are falling short of the amount of canopy coverage needed to effectively reduce city temperatures.
