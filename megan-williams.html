Fash Fashion

Environment

Fast Fashion is devastating our planet through significant negative impacts on the environment, labor practices, and society. Our water, land, and air are becoming so polluted that we are reaching a point of no return.


These companies are producing clothing with chemicals and materials so toxic that they cannot be broken down or reused, the clothing dyes are seeping into our own water systems poisoning our drinking water, the clothes that cannot be recycled have to be burned, and all of these cheap clothing items are made by abused workers, many of which are women and children. Thrifting and recycling cannot fix this problem. We have to make these companies be held accountable through action and laws that prevent the blatant disregard for life in all forms.
