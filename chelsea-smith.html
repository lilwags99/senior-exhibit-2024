Chelsea Smith

instagram.com/cksmith.design

In the vibrant city of San Diego, California, Chelsea Smith flourishes as a versatile creative force. A Freelance Artist, adept Graphic Designer, and aspiring writer, she is poised to graduate with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Graphic Design. Recognizing the boundless growth opportunities, Chelsea endeavors to further hone her artistic and design acumen by pursuing an additional Bachelor of Arts degree in Visual Communication Design at Arizona State University. Fueled by her passion for storytelling and admiration for beloved shows and characters, Chelsea channels her creativity into crafting captivating fan works. From meticulously designed prints to charming keychains, she shares her interpretations with fellow enthusiasts, offering them a piece of her artistic vision to cherish.

Wealth Inequality

Economic

Wealth inequality and inflation are two intertwined economic phenomena that profoundly impact societies worldwide. As wealth inequality widens, with a disproportionate accumulation of resources among a select few, inflation exacerbates the financial burdens faced by those with limited means. Rising prices for essentials like housing, education, and healthcare disproportionately affect low-income individuals and families, further deepening the chasm between the affluent and the marginalized.


Moreover, inflation erodes the purchasing power of fixed incomes, leaving vulnerable populations struggling to make ends meet. Addressing both wealth inequality and inflation demands comprehensive strategies that promote equitable economic growth, access to education and healthcare, and progressive taxation policies to redistribute resources more fairly, ultimately fostering inclusive prosperity for all.
