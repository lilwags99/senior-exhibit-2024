Social Media and the Adolescent Mind

Mental Health

Social media is something that has become ingrained in our culture regardless of age, but it has especially become a major part of teenage life as teenagers spend hours on platforms daily. While these platforms are advertised as ways to connect with peers and loved ones, it is becoming more and more clear that these platforms are a detriment to adolescent mental health.


Depression and anxiety have skyrocketed and addictive behaviors are commonplace. These mental health issues are severe and have major effects on the minds of these young individuals. We need to be aware of how social media platforms are molding and affecting adolescent mental health.
