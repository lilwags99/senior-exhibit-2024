The Class of 2024

On behalf of the 2024 Visual Communication Design Senior Class, we extend our warmest welcome to “Change Directions.” Together we have tread revolutionary grounds for this program and now represent the largest class in VCD history. With 77 students— some of whom join us from across the country and around the globe — we are ASU’s first joint-graduating class of in-person and online graphic design students.

This new direction for the ASU graphic design community began in 2020, and over a four-year journey of overcoming challenges and pushing boundaries, we are celebrating the culmination of our undergraduate experience at this year’s Annual Senior Exhibition. “Change Directions” celebrates growth, knowledge, and hope through design — all for a more promising future.
