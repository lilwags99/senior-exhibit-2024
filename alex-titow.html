Alex Titow

Alexander Titow has always had a passion for storytelling. This first manifested in a love of writing/reading, but he always enjoyed the visual representation of these works when they appeared. This has led to another hobby of doodling in his youth, and now it has blossomed into a love of design. He learned there are other ways to tell a story beyond words, and that has been the biggest development in how he approaches storytelling. Now he sees visual language as another great, and intrinsic way humans have told stories for generations, and he is excited to see where else this takes him!
